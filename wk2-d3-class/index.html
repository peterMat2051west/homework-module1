Welcome

Experience the magic of Central Park from a horse-drawn carriage. Our rides take you through the most scenic paths of the park, from the iconic Bethesda Fountain to the peaceful trails along the lake.

Available year-round, with special winter rides through snow-covered paths. Perfect for couples, families, or anyone looking to enjoy New York City at a slower pace.
