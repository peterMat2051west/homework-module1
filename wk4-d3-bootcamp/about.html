About Splashes 🧽

Get to know our underwater crew!

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Bikini Bottom, Splashes was born when SpongeBob SquarePants realized that not everyone was as comfortable in the water as he was. After watching his best friend Patrick struggle to float (despite living under the sea), SpongeBob gathered the best swimmers in town to create a swimming school like no other.

Today, Splashes is the premier children's swimming academy in all of Bikini Bottom. Our team of certified instructors brings passion, patience, and a whole lot of fun to every lesson. Whether your child is touching water for the first time or training for the Bikini Bottom Junior Olympics, we have a class for them!
